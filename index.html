Guide

How the hiring pipeline works and scoring reference

🚀 Quick Start — 3 Steps
1
Add the applicant
Click + New Applicant in the sidebar and fill in their name, email, and position.
2
Send & track
Open Tracker, click their name — a "Next step" banner tells you exactly what to do at every stage.
3
Decide
Once assessments, EMM, and interview are done, record the final decision on their Decision tab.
Pipeline Stages
Which list an applicant follows depends on their Department. Numbers on the Tracker/Board mean different things for each track — that's expected.
Track A — AP/AR (Operations)
1
Application Received
Applicant added to the system — already screened before they were entered.
2
HR Interview Done — Send Assessments
HR already interviewed them. Now send GRIT + Values, plus EMM if the role requires it.
3
Assessments Sent
Waiting for GRIT/Values (and EMM, if required) to come back.
4
Assessments Passed — Operations Manager Interview Needed
They passed their tests. The Operations Manager needs to set up an interview.
5
Operations Manager Interview — In Progress
The Operations Manager is interviewing them now, or it’s about to happen.
6
Interview Passed — Send to Client
They passed the interview. Go send this candidate to the external client now.
7
Waiting on Client’s Decision
Already sent to the client — nothing to do until they respond.
8
Job Offer Sent
Client approved the candidate; ABBSS sends the formal offer.
9
Hired
Offer accepted.
Track B — Sales & Marketing
1
Application Received
Applicant added to the system — already screened before they were entered.
2
Assessments Sent
GRIT + Values are emailed out (no EMM on this track).
3
Preliminary Interview (HR) Needed
They passed their tests. HR needs to set up an interview.
4
Preliminary Interview (HR) — In Progress
HR is interviewing them now, or it’s about to happen.
5
Second Interview (Project Manager) — In Progress
The Project Manager (Frances) is interviewing them now.
6
Final Interview (CEO) — In Progress
The CEO is interviewing them now.
7
Job Offer Sent
CEO approved; ABBSS sends the offer directly — no external client involved on this track.
8
Hired
Offer accepted.

Connect to Google Sheets for shared data across devices

Google Sheets Sync
Not connected

Once connected, all applicant records sync automatically to your Google Sheet so every HR staff member sees the same data in real time. GRIT and Values scores are pulled in automatically by email match.

Recalculate Pipeline Stages

The pipeline was rebuilt with new, more detailed stages. Applicants added before this update kept their old stage number, which now means something different — so some records may show the wrong step (e.g. someone already at the Ops interview showing as still in screening). Run this once to re-check every applicant's actual GRIT/Values/EMM/interview data and correct their stage. It uses your real answers as the source of truth, not the old stored number, and applies the same fix to every applicant automatically — you shouldn't need to fix people one at a time.
